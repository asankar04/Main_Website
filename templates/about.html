About Me

Hi! I’m Anit Sankar, a dedicated and adaptable Computer Science student at the University of Alabama at Birmingham with a minor in Business Administration. With a perfect GPA and a keen interest in both technology and business, I’m driven by a passion for problem-solving and innovation. My hands-on experience includes interning as a Software Engineer at ShipShape Urban Farms, where I enhanced data collection efficiency and automated workflows, and as a Web Developer at CircleBox, where I accelerated website development and elevated its design.

I’ve also enjoyed working on my own projects, like a password generator website using ReactJS, which lets users create strong, customized passwords. Outside of my academics and projects, I serve as a Programming Teaching Assistant in the Microsoft TEALS Program, where I guide students in honing their fundamental programming skills, and I serve as the Public Relations Chair for the Association of Computer Machinery, where I actively organize events and boost our online presence through social media.

I’m excited about the endless possibilities in tech and eager to make a positive impact through my work.
